# PART III - CYBER INCIDENT RESPONSE TEAMS

## 6. Zambia Cyber Incident Response Team

<ol type="1">
  <li>The Agency shall constitute the Zambia Cyber Incident Response Team which shall—
    <ol type="a">
      <li>be the first point of contact with reference to the handling of cyber incidents and communication between local, regional and international cyber security incident response teams;</li>
      <li>provide incident response and management services in a coordinated manner through established industry standard policies and procedures to manage threats associated with cyber incidents;</li>
      <li>provide alerts and warnings on the latest cyber threats and vulnerabilities which may impact the public and the private sector;</li>
      <li>undertake national cyber security risk assessments;</li>
      <li>assess and coordinate the work of sectoral cyber incident response teams within the public and private sector;</li>
      <li>participate in regional and international computer emergency response team groups; and</li>
      <li>establish a cyber security incident monitoring and response system.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <li>The Agency shall determine the composition, terms of reference, tenure and procedures of the Zambia Cyber Incident Response Team.</li>
</ol>

## 7. Constitution of sectoral cyber incident response teams

<ol type="1">
  <li>The Agency shall, by notice in the Gazette, require a sector to constitute a sectoral cyber incident response team.</li>
  <li>The notice referred to under subsection (1), shall specify—
    <ol type="a">
      <li>the composition of the sectoral cyber incident response team; and</li>
      <li>the institution to coordinate the sectoral cyber incident response team.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <li>The Agency shall, in requiring a sector to constitute a sectoral cyber incident response team, take into account—
    <ol type="a">
      <li>the needs and criticality of a sector;</li>
      <li>developments in respect of cyber security in the Republic; and</li>
      <li>any other factors that the Agency may determine.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <li>A sectoral cyber incident response team shall—
    <ol type="a">
      <li>collect and collate cyber security incidents; and</li>
      <li>co-ordinate responses to cyber security incidents within the sectors.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <li>The Agency shall oversee the operations of a sectoral cyber incident response team constituted under this section.</li>
  <li>A sectoral cyber incident response team shall bear the cost of establishing and operationalising that cyber incident response team.</li>
  <li>A sectoral cyber incident response team shall submit a report on the operations of that sectoral cyber incident response team to the Agency in a manner determined by the Agency.</li>
</ol>
